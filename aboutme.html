Shib S. Sahoo

I have an eclectic background spanning Design Research, Communication Design and Product Design. I'm a cross disciplinary designer whose academic interests lie in the realm of Human-Computer Interaction. My focus areas include, but are not limited to Human Factors in design, branding and logo design, interactive product design and graphic arts.
