Udghosh Branding

By Shib S. Sahoo

I had an opportunity to design the official logo for annual Sports festival Udghosh. The logo is combination of a horse which stands for speed and passion and the rising man represents the uplifting spirit of the festival.The logo is also a complimentary combination of red and blue colors depicts the simultaneous mixture of fire red- for passion and energy and ice blue- patience and calmness required for sportsman.
