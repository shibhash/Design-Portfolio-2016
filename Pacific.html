Pacific Smoke Version 1.0

Game design | Motion Graphics | 3D Animation | VFX | Sound design

Game design

By Shib S. Sahoo



The project began in late summers. The aim was to develop a third person story-telling based game .
The project began with simple concept sketches while I was in train. I got inspired from pacific battle, World War II. The story revolves around various islands of Pacific Ocean allied power ship disappearing in strange manner. The allied (United States, Britain, Australia) thought of it was Empire of Japan who carried out mysterious experimental weapon . But was it really ?

Phase-I started with a theme decision and story-boarding. I tested few concepts on paper . The initial phase worked out quite well. I decided to start with character and arena view first. The earlier high angle shots are mostly based on helicopter flying over pacific ocean, as the story progressed taget cameras were used to capure the North Kiribati ocean region and the ship. I started experimenting on few camera angles, Meanwhile I came up with an idea of locking the camera focus to the dummy object and camera to a spline which eventually gave me more interesting shots.








Story board

A single player game mode, Game consists of two level Initial level comes with a terrain map with enemy base camp. In Second level Player attacks the heavily guarded enemy base camp
