Design Singularity an artificial creative

Form generation | artificial intelligence

I had this idea "what could be the culmination point of visual arts and design?" I settled down for a concept to work on, which I later named Design singularity.

Inspiration

AR | VR | AI

Inspiration I - We have been reading quite a lot about AR, VR and mixed reality, google glass, Microsoft hololens lately. It just gives me feeling that we have started capturing our memories, experiences, and emotions in a more quantitative and tangible form; as if we can transcend ourselves into an alternate reality of past, future or may be a completely surreal entity any time we want.
Inspiration II - I came across the few photo editing apps like prisma, google dream projects I found the process brilliant. you teach machine to recognise distinctive features and styles of famous artist like picasso or van gogh, collect the data points , take a secondary image and apply your algorithm. Viola! the next morning you wake up, your facebook wall doesn't look the way it did.
It just gives me feeling that we have started capturing our memories, experiences, and emotions in a more quantitive and tangible form

Artificial

Creativity

"Could we create an intelligent system, for now what I call is an ARTIFICIAL CREATIVE?"

We, as designers follow some specific thought process to reach to a certain decision(But, of course there are exception who don't play by the rule!). Let me put it more clearly, let's say my typical client gives me design brief where he says "I want a sporty logo, with a touch of tradition , it would be great if you incorporate our mascot in it". At first, it seems like a nightmare to any graphic designer. humans are so used to their muscle memory and the way they perceive a problem tha after a certain time they find it hard to create a disruptive form, a divine form that no human can ever think of with the given his IQ. So how could we create distruptive designs?

Here comes the Artificial Creative.We give inputs like masculine form(more edge and diagonal),  sporty (spontaneous line quality) , traditional (a color scheme picked of from traditional set of images). Parameters can vary from 5 to 50.our artificial creative could quickly generate forms either 2D or 3D, which can act as a first level thought for disruptive form generation.And can augment human creativity. possibilities are endless. This is what I call the culmination of creativity and visual thinking A design singularity >
