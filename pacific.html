Pacific Smoke Version 1.0

Game design | Motion Graphics | 3D Animation | VFX | Sound design

Game design

By Shib S. Sahoo



Overview

The project began in late summers. My aim was to develop a third person story-telling based game battle game. I began with simple concept sketches while I was travelling in train back to college. The game inspiration was derived from pacific battle,World War II. A single player game mode, Game consists of two levels, Initial level comes with a terrain map with enemy base camps and second level moves around is based on pacific ocean battle. Here is a cinematic preview of the game










Initial stages

I sketched few concepts on paper and quickly moved into concept art development and storyboarding. As the initial phase worked out quite well, I decided to start with character and game arena design first. The earlier high angle shots are mostly based on helicopter flying over pacific ocean, as the story progressed target cameras were used to capture the North Kiribati ocean region and the ship. I started experimenting on few camera angles, Meanwhile I came up with an idea of locking the camera focus to the dummy object and camera to a spline which eventually turned out to be interesting.

Story board

The story revolves around various islands of Pacific Ocean allied power ship disappearing in a mysterious way. The allied (United States, Britain, Australia) thought of it was Empire of Japan who carried out mysterious experimental weapon. But it turned out to be something else !







Camera and Environment Setup

Daylight, Composition, Target camera, spline constraint setup and animation




Animation

Key frame animation, Max particle effects, Simulation and Curve editing in Autodesk 3ds Max





Post processing

Special effects (using Chroma key, Masking, Particle effects) and Color correction in Adobe After Effects and Premiere Pro
