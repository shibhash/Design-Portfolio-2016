Pacific Smoke Version 1.0

Game design | Motion Graphics | 3D Animation | VFX | Sound design

Overview

Pacific smoke

The project began in late summers. My aim was to develop a third person story-telling based battle game. I began with simple concept sketches while I was travelling in train back to college. Link to the cinematic preview of the game below-

I was inspired by Pacific battle, World War-II. Game consists of two levels, Initial level comes with a terrain map with enemy base camps and second level revolves around ship disappearance in Pacific.








Initial stages

Planning and execution

I sketched few concepts on paper and quickly moved into concept art development and storyboarding. As the initial phase worked out quite well, I decided to start with character and game arena design first. The earlier high angle shots are mostly based on helicopter flying over pacific ocean

But as the story progressed target cameras were used to capture the North Kiribati ocean region and the ship. I started experimenting on few camera angles. Meanwhile, I came up with an idea of locking the camera focus to the dummy object and camera to a spline which eventually turned out to be an interesting approach .

Storyboarding

Concept sketches

The story revolves around various islands of Pacific Ocean allied power ship disappearing in a mysterious way. The allied (United States, Britain, Australia) thought of it was Empire of Japan who carried out mysterious experimental weapon. But it turned out to be something else !
