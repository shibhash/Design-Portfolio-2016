Retractable Wolverine Claws

Manufacturing process Project

Guide: Dr. Kantesh Balani, IIT Kanpur
Group project to make Retractable Wolverine claws using various Manufacturing Processes. Wolverine claws could be ejected out when required and then retracted back. These claws were prepared using casting, welding, metal forming and brazing manufacturing processes
